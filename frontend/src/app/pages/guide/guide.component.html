Guide to Weaving Words

Hear Ye! Hear Ye!

Greetings, noble scribes and word weavers! Gather 'round, gather 'round, for I shall regale you with the secrets of using TaleThread, a land where your tales and poems come to life. Fear not, for this guide is crafted in the whimsical tongue of yore, fit for villagers and wanderers alike.

The First Step: Your Profile Page

In this humble abode, you will see your profile, friend list, and the tapestry of your literary journey. Here, you can:

  • View and Edit Your Profile: Modify your details as you see fit.
  • Friend List: Behold your comrades, those who share your passion for the quill.

The Grand Bazaar: Threads Page

Enter the bustling marketplace of stories and poems, where threads of words await your perusal. On this page, you will:

  • Explore Threads: Browse the myriad tales and poems crafted by fellow scribes.
  • Join the Fray: Contribute your words to ongoing threads.

Crafting Your Masterpiece: Create Thread

Ah, the forge of creativity! Here, you will begin your own thread, be it a tale or a poem. Remember:

  • Tokens: It costs 50 tokens to start a thread. Fear not, for every new account is blessed with 500 tokens.
  • Set Contributors and Word Limits: Decide how many contributors may join and the maximum words per contribution. Any exceeding words shall vanish like the morning mist.

The Vault: Saved Threads

Within these hallowed halls lie the threads you have chosen to save. Here, you can:

  • Review Your Favorites: Revisit the threads that have captured your heart.
  • Contribute Anew: Continue your participation in these beloved threads.

The Ancient Scroll: How to use Talethread

1. The Beginning of Your Journey

  • Registering: Upon joining our humble village, you are gifted 500 tokens. Use them wisely!
  • Starting a Thread: Spend 50 inkTokens to ignite the spark of a new tale or poem. Set your preferred number of contributors and word limit, for they are the loom and shuttle of your thread.

2. Weaving Contributions

  • Gaining Inktokens: For every contribution, earn 50 inktokens. The quill rewards the diligent!
  • Likes and Dislikes: A like grants you 25 inktokens, while a dislike takes away 25. The village folk are kind yet honest in their judgments.

3. The Companions of Your Quest

  • Adding Friends: Seek out fellow scribes and add them to your friend list. A tale shared is a tale cherished.
  • Setting Favorite Genres: Mark your favorite genres and sort threads accordingly. Let the stories align with your heart's desire.
Thus ends our tale, noble scribe. With this knowledge, go forth and weave the threads of your imagination into the grand tapestry of TaleThread. May your words be ever flowing and your tokens ever plentiful!
